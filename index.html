🌡 Hỏi Thời tiết & Lý do (Why - Because)

• What's the weather like? → It's windy.

• Don't put on your coat. Why? → Because it's hot.

• Wear a sun hat. Why? → Because it's sunny.

• Let's go home. Why? → Because it's rainy.

• Do/Does...? Do you like...? / Does he like...? [cite: 5, 6]

🕒 Giờ giấc & Thói quen

• What time is it? → It's seven o'clock.

• What time do you get up? → I get up at seven o'clock.

• What time does he/she + Vs/es? → He goes to bed at nine o'clock.

🧭 Chỉ đường & Vị trí [cite: 3, 6]

• How do we get to the hospital? → Turn left/right, Go straight.

• Vị trí: Next to (Kế bên), Opposite (Đối diện), Between (Ở giữa).

⏳ Quá khứ đơn (To Be)

• I / He / She / It: was / wasn't (e.g., I was cute).

• You / We / They: were / weren't (e.g., We were young).
